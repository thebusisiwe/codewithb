Signing Up To FNB App Academy

Hi

So you're here because you've heard about the fnb app academy and want to sign up. Applications have closed, however, the curriculum has been made public so you can still learn to code alongside this year's cohort. Follow these steps and you should be set!

Fill in the form on the IT varsity website and come back here when you are done.

Once you've submitted your registration form you need to go to the sign-in page and click on "forgot password" then fill in the email you used to register. IT varsity will then send you an email so you can set a passowrd and log in.

If you are experiencing problems with re-setting your password, check that:

    • the email for resetting the passowrd is not in your spam folder
      you've only filled in the email portion and the other one is empty

    • You will now receive the lessons delivered to your email inbox.

    Once you have access to the lessons, you can start coding. The lessons are designed to be self-paced, so you can take your time to understand each concept before moving on.

    Remember, coding is a journey, and it's okay to take your time. Don't rush through the lessons. Make sure you understand each concept before moving on to the next one.

    If you have any questions or need help, you can reach out to the FNB App Academy team through their support channels. They are there to help you succeed.

    Good luck on your coding journey!
